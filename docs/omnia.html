Omnia Focus

Volume I – 22 July 2024

Omnia is an independent, multidisciplinary, open-access journal dedicated to enhancing the communication of science-related topics.

The Focus section has been created to distil the main highlights of featured research articles into concise summaries of 700-800 words (or one A4 page in the current layout). This approach aims to make scientific language more accessible, provide readers with a clear overview, and encourage them to explore the original articles more deeply.

In this first Volume (July 2024), a collection of research highlights primarily related to the macro category of plants is presented. The highlights are available as blog posts and in a convenient printable format (PDF).
